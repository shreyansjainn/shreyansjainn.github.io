I am self taught Machine Learning Practitioner currently working as a Senior Data Scientist in GEP's India AI Team. The views expressed on this blog are personal opinions, and do not reflect the opinions of my employer.

One of my main interest area is deep reinforcement learning, although in my work, i've dabbled mainly into use cases like personalization, forecasting and search engines. I like thinking about how we can design RL agents which can be deployed and scaled safely to the real world .

Although I'm a Civil Engineering graduate, math was always one of those subjects i used to turn towards for stress-busting during school days. So no matter how corny or cliched it may sound, but my decision to switch careers to Machine Learning can be attributed to my love of that beautiful subject.

This site double as my personal blog. Here I post about machine learning, observations about life or things around me and any topic which might be too close to my heart.

In my free time (which lately has been a scarce resource), I dabble into photography, playing keyboard, listening to classic rock. Recently I've started learning chess, and I'm quite enjoying the process.
